PRIVACY POLICY FOR NRCNATURALAPP

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

INTRODUCTION

Atlas Development ("we," "our," or "us") operates the NrcNaturalApp mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Information Automatically Collected
We may collect certain information automatically when you use the App, including:
- Device information (device type, operating system version)
- App usage data (features used, time spent in app)
- Crash logs and performance data
- Advertising identifiers (for personalized ads)

The App does NOT track your precise location or access your contacts.

Information Stored Locally
The App stores the following information locally on your device only:

1. User Preferences
   - Theme preference (light mode or dark mode)
   - Volume level settings (5 levels: Muted, Low, Medium, High, Amplified)
   - Language preference (English, Turkish, and other supported languages)
   - Selected mood/ambient background preference (Riverside, Mountain, On The Cloud, Desert, Under the Sea)

2. User-Generated Content
   - Notes that you create and save in the Notes section
   - These notes are stored locally and never transmitted to any server unless you explicitly enable cloud backup features

3. Purchase Information
   - Records of in-app purchases (stored locally and synced with Google Play)
   - Premium feature unlocks and subscription status

All preference data is stored using SharedPreferences on your device. Purchase data is managed by Google Play Store.

DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

Local Storage
- User preferences and notes are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences API
- Data is not encrypted in local storage
- You can clear all app data at any time through your device's Settings > Apps > NrcNaturalApp > Storage > Clear Data

Cloud Storage
- Purchase history is stored and managed by Google Play Store
- We do not operate our own servers for user data storage
- If we implement cloud sync features in the future, data will be encrypted in transit

Backup
- Android's backup service may back up your app preferences to your Google account if you have Android Backup enabled
- This is controlled by your device settings, not by the App
- Notes may be included in the backup scope

DATA SECURITY
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

PERMISSIONS USED

Internet Access (INTERNET)
- Purpose: To download and cache Google Fonts, display ads, and process in-app purchases
- Data Transmitted: Font requests, ad requests, purchase verification requests
- Note: Personal identifying information is not transmitted without your consent

Audio/Media Access (READ_MEDIA_AUDIO for Android 13+, READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE for older versions)
- Purpose: To play ambient nature sounds stored in the app's assets
- Note: The App only accesses its own bundled audio files, not your personal media

Vibration (VIBRATE)
- Purpose: For notification features and haptic feedback
- Current Use: May be used for timer alerts and notifications

Network State (ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE)
- Purpose: To check internet connectivity before loading ads or processing purchases
- Note: Only checks if internet is available, does not track your browsing
